About

I am a Data & BI Analyst dedicated to architecting data systems that drive executive clarity across diverse industries. With 3+ years of experience — ranging from enterprise consulting at global firms to higher education analytics — I specialize in transforming fragmented, messy datasets into high-fidelity pipelines and dashboards.

My work sits at the intersection of diagnosis, validation, and translation. Leveraging my M.S. in Computer Science, I engineer automated SQL and Python workflows that surface the signal within the noise. I don’t just ship dashboards — I build KPI governance frameworks that ensure data integrity and provide a single source of truth for leadership.

My approach is strictly investigation-first. Whether I’m modeling $801M in cost-savings on 9.66M Medicare claims or diagnosing R$3.8M in recoverable revenue across 93K e-commerce customers, I focus on de-risking business decisions through rigorous, end-to-end analysis.
